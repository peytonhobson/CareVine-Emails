The Meeting Place for Families and Private Care

CareVine is a new online platform that connects patients and families with local private caregivers, aiming to keep seniors at home longer. CareVine allows clients to choose their caregivers, empowering them to maintain control over their care and cost. By providing a transparent platform where rates are set by the caregivers themselves, it allows for more competitive pricing as well as flexibility.

Our goal at CareVine is to provide a place where both parties benefit through their own agency. We believe in that wholeheartedly.
